Step 1: Download Your Brightpearl Inventory Summary

To get your on-hand quantities from Brightpearl, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to Brightpearl and navigate to Inventory → Reports → Inventory Summary.
  2. Set the warehouse filter to your store location.
  3. Click Export to CSV and save the file to your computer.
  4. The file will contain your current on-hand quantities — you'll upload it in Step 2 below.

This content will be updated with screenshots and exact navigation steps.

Step 2: Upload Your Inventory Summary

Select your Brightpearl Inventory Summary CSV and click Upload. This will be used to compare against your physical count.
