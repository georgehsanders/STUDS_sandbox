Step 1: Brightpearl Inventory Summary

Download a Brightpearl Inventory Summary for your studio and upload it here.

Here's how:

  1. Login to Brightpearl.
  2. Click "Products"
  3. Click "Inventory Summary"
  4. Go to the "warehouse" dropdown menu and select your studio.
  5. Click "Filter".
  6. Click "Export" to download your studio's inventory summary.

Once the file is downloaded, drop it into the upload box below.

Step 4: Reconcile Your Variances

In the OmniCounts Dashboard, go to "Reports" and download your Count Report for this week. Then upload it below.

Once uploaded, you'll see a variance table comparing your Brightpearl on-hand quantities (from Step 1) against the counts you just completed in OmniCounts. You can edit the Counted column directly if you need to correct a count — the variance will recalculate on screen.

Step 5: Check for Major Variances

Look over your variance table. If you see something that looks off, go recount that SKU and confirm the quantity matches.

Step 6: Make Your Brightpearl Adjustments

Use the variance report from Step 4 to make all necessary adjustments directly in Brightpearl.

Once all adjustments have been made, come back here and continue to the last step.

After completing your adjustments in Brightpearl, download a fresh Inventory Summary Report and upload it below. We'll crosscheck it against your reported variances to confirm everything was accurately accounted for.
